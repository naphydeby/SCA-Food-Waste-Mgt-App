Zero Hunger, One of Lagos' Most sustainable Goal

Our aim is to make sure that no Lagosian goes hungry

Every day, thousands of people in Lagos struggle to find their next meal, while tons of food go to waste. At Food Salvage, we bridge this gap by rescuing surplus food and redistributing it to those in need. With your support, we can turn excess into hope, waste into nourishment, and hunger into fullness. Your donation isn't just a gift—it’s a lifeline.
